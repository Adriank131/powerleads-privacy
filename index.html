Who we are

Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone.

How we use location

Technician location is used to assign the nearest technician and to provide customers with a public tracking link (live map + ETA). The app may update location in the background to keep ETA accurate while the technician is en route.

Sharing

  • We share the technician’s location only with the service customer via the tracking link.
  • Vendors (hosting, messaging, maps) process data under confidentiality agreements.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Legal basis

We process data to perform the requested service, for legitimate interests (logistics and safety), and to comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Retention

Live location is retained for the time needed to operate and trace the service. Historical records and evidence (photos/videos) are retained per operational and legal needs.

Security
